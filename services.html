Primary Sales / New property Consultancy

Our primary focus is on facilitating successful property transactions while providing exceptional service to clients. The key elements of primary sales for a real estate agent include:

  • Client Consultation
  • Property Valuation
  • Property Listings
  • Marketing Strategies
  • Client Education
  • Legal Compliance
  • Post-Sale Support

Resale Transaction

When it comes to a resale transaction, We at Azuma Realty engage in a specialised set of activities to facilitate the smooth and successful resale of a property. Here are the key steps involved in a resale transaction:

  • Property Assessment
  • Market Analysis
  • Seller Consultation
  • Marketing and Listing
  • Negotiation
  • Contract and Paperwork
  • Due Diligence
  • Closing Coordination

Rental Transaction

In handling rental transactions, We at Azuma Realty plays a crucial role in connecting landlords with suitable tenants. The process involves a series of steps to ensure a smooth and mutually beneficial rental experience for both property owners and tenants:

  • Landlord Consultation
  • Property Assessment
  • Market Analysis
  • Property Marketing
  • Tenant Screening
  • Property Showings
  • Lease Agreement
  • Security Deposits & Rent Collection
  • Move-In Coordination
  • Ongoing Communication
  • Lease Renewals & Terminations

Home Loan Assistance

At Azuma Realty, We understand the importance of assisting clients with home loan processes to make their property transactions more accessible. Here's how Azuma Realty's real estate agents provide home loan assistance:

  • Financial Consultation
  • Lender Referrals
  • Loan Pre-Approval Assistance
  • Educating Clients on Loan Options
  • Collaboration with Lenders
  • Documentation Assistance
  • Monitoring Loan Progress
  • Coordination with Closing Process
  • Post-Closing Support
